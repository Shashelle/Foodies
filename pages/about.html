Who? What? When? Where? Why?

The 'FOODIES' actually started off in October 2018 as an all-female rock band called The 'Femmies' but with no musical talent the band quickly dissmembered... (just one month after their formation)

Saddened by this harsh reality, four of the original band mambers ANNA, CHARLOTTE, MARISSA and RACHELLE found comfort through eating (and drinking). And through this new found love of food (and drink) that they were able to fill (literally) the gaping void in their souls from their music.

Now, one month on from the band's 'fall'(December 2018) they have re-grouped and 'risen' into a new collective, called the 'FOODIES'
